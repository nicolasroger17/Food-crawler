<p>A <b>cookie/biscuit sandwich</b> is a type of <a href="/wiki/Biscuit" title="Biscuit">biscuit</a> consisting of two cookies/biscuits, between which is a filling.<sup id="cite_ref-Crocker_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Crocker-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-HP_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HP-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Randle_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Randle-3"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Morgan_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Morgan-4"><span>[</span>4<span>]</span></a></sup> Many types of fillings are used, such as <a href="/wiki/Ganache" title="Ganache">ganache</a>, <a href="/wiki/Buttercream" title="Buttercream">buttercream</a>, chocolate, cream cheese, jam, peanut butter, <a href="/wiki/Lemon_curd" title="Lemon curd" class="mw-redirect">lemon curd</a> and ice cream,<sup id="cite_ref-Crocker_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Crocker-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-HP_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HP-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Morgan_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Morgan-4"><span>[</span>4<span>]</span></a></sup> among others. The cookie/biscuit version of the <a href="/wiki/Whoopie_pie" title="Whoopie pie">whoopie pie</a> is a type of cookie/biscuit sandwich.<sup id="cite_ref-Crocker_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Crocker-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
